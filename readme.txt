== Description ==

*AdSense Immediately!* is a simple way to generate revenue from your blog using Google AdSense. AdSense Immediately! is a simple plugin to ad adsense ads into the body of your content. It can add one, two or three adsense block to your article body in any google ads format.  It alos supports various alignments of your ads with the article and suppressing ads on certain types of pages.  


== Other Notes ==
Adsense Immediately! is based on the Adsense Now plugin and incorporates some features from Adsense manger plugin.
